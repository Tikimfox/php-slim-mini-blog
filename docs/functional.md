## Documentation - app functions

## Views
1. Home
    - Create articles
    - View other articles
2. Blog details (choose blog)
    - Comments
    - Comment form

## Use scenarios
- Create New articles by accessing the home page
- Viewing other articles by scrolling down the home page.
- To add a comment: open a desired article, scroll down
  and fill in the form (name, comment)
- To reply to someone's comment: press reply button (the arrow)
  at the desired comment, fill in the other form (not the one for
  adding new comment) by typing your name and the comment itself. 
  Note: the reply form will be selected automatically.
